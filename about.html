Our Story

Tradition, Quality, and Trust Since 1985

Welcome to Chaurasiya Pan Dukan

Founded in 1985 by Shri Ramashish Chaurasiya, our humble pan shop started as a small stall in the bustling streets of saptri. What began as a means to support a family soon grew into a beloved local institution, known for its authentic flavors and quality products.

Over the decades, we've maintained our traditional preparation methods while adapting to modern hygiene standards. Our secret family recipes, passed down through generations, continue to delight customers who come from near and far.

Our Philosophy

At Chaurasiya Pan Dukan, we believe in:

  • Using only the freshest, highest quality ingredients
  • Maintaining traditional preparation methods
  • Providing exceptional customer service
  • Giving back to our community
Shri Ram Chaurasiya - Founder (1985)
